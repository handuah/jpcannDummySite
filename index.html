Company Profile

We are a management and capacity building consultancy company.We provide business advisory services, accountancy and finance, and building the capacity of individuals, institutions and corporate bodies in Ghana and beyond. Our services are mainly tailor-made to the needs and environment of our clients business.

These are mainly in the areas of corporate capacity development and business advisory services mainly to the SME sector of the Ghanaian economy and also to the public sector organizations. Our team of experts is mainly specialized in different disciplines such as banking and finance, SME development, market research, auditing, risk management, human resource and general business advisory.

We are more than willing to discuss your needs with you in either corporate training, accountancy and business advisory areas.
