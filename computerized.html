Computerized Accounting Services

At JPCann Associates Limited (JAL) we specialize in helping organisations choose and implement accounting solutions to automate their transactions and reporting. We offer both desktop and *cloud based*solutions that can easily be integrated into other applications. We will support this effort through choosing softwares, design and customisation, implementation, training and coaching, remote support services.

Some of our portfolio includes:

  • USAID – SFMP Project – Computerized Accounting Systems Using QuickBooks (2015);
  • Computerization of six (6) Partners of the USAID-SFMP Project (2015)
  • QuickBooks Computerization for Legal Ink Ghana Ltd (2014)
  • Computerized Accounting – HelpAge Ghana, Osu Accra, : QuickBooks Computerization Project (2014)
  • International Resource Centre (IRC), Dzorwulu, Accra – QuickBooks Computerization Project (2015)
  • Computerization of Accounting Systems of Damango Development Organisation (DDO) under the Catholic Bishop Secretariat of Damango, Tamale
  • Computerization of the Accounting Systems of Navorongo – Bolgatanga Catholic Development Organisation (NABOCADO)
