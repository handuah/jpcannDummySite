Vision & Mission
Our Vision
JAL’s vision is to build capacity, strengthen and improve performance of institutions in developing and emerging markets especially in the sub-Saharan region of Africa.
Our Mission
JAL’s key mission is to transform the private and public sectors of emerging economies through the provision of consultancy and assurance services aimed at building and strengthening the capacities of both the private and public sector organizations in achieving improved performance.
Our Core Values

Social Enterpreneurship

The social benefits of our work is more important than the desire to make profit

Improved Performance

Our services are aimed at improving client’s performance, systems and human capacity.

Professionalism

Our strength lies in our professionalism towards our clients

Transformation

Our belief is in transforming our societies and raising leaders

Local Emphasis

Local solutions but international perspective in building capacities

Business Advisory Services

Business Advisory Services (BAS) to the SMEs, public sector organisations, donor agencies and partners

Our focus is to offer professional services aimed at given our clients value for money through efficient, effective and economical services aimed at increasing service and product excellence and achieving results.
